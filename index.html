Dungeon!

Dungeon: A game inspired by Japanese RPG's and dungeon crawlers. This game took inspiration from games like Legend of Zelda and Pokemon. In this game the player spawns in X location and his job is to traverse his way through enemies out of the dungeon. Along the way he may encounter loot, ninjas, dragons, or other enemies! The idea for this game was pitched by me and alongside Gary Schreiner we completed this project. No Game engine was used; everything was programmed in C++ using Nokia's Qt. MySQL was also used to save any game state so that the player can stop and pick up where he left off at any time.
